### ğŸ›¡ï¸ **The Importance of Strong Passwords**
- **Dictionary Attacks**: Hackers use lists of common words to guess passwords.
- **Brute Force Attacks**: Hackers try every possible combination of characters to crack your password.

### ğŸ§® **Password Complexity**
- **Four-Digit Passcodes**: Only 10,000 possible combinations, making them easy to crack.
- **Four-Letter Passwords**: Using uppercase and lowercase letters increases the complexity to 52^4 (7 million possibilities).
- **Four-Character Passwords**: Including letters, numbers, and punctuation increases the complexity to 94^4 (78 million possibilities).
- **Eight-Character Passwords**: With 94^8 possibilities, the complexity skyrockets to 6 quadrillion combinations, making it much harder to crack.
